# Interview deep dive

## Why is account-open completion the primary metric?
Because the treatment is an onboarding-flow redesign, so completion rate is the cleanest short-term causal readout.

## Why keep guardrails?
Because a higher completion rate is not a good win if it comes with worse user quality or higher service burden.

## Why add first-deposit rate?
Because completion alone can overstate business value. In this case, 7-day first-deposit rate also increased by **1.94 ppt**.

## Where is the main friction reduction?
- Submit Basic Info: +7.14 ppt
- Risk Assessment Start: +4.40 ppt
- Risk Assessment Complete: +5.11 ppt
